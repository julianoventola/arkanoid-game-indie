How to play:

  • "a" or Arrow Left - to move left

  • "d" or Arrow Right - to move left

  • Brick Green - takes 1 hit to destroy

  • Brick Yellow - takes 2 hits to destroy

  • Brick Blue - takes 2 hits to destroy

  • Brick Purple - takes 3 hits to destroy
